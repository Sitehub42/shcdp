About Us

At Small Harbour Community Development Projects NPC, hereinafter referred to as SHCDP, we are dedicated to transforming small harbor communities in Hout Bay, Cape Town. Our mission is to uplift and empower these communities through entrepreneurship, skills development, and environmental stewardship, fostering sustainable growth and well-being.

We bring extensive expertise in Community Development, Entrepreneurship, Skills Training, and Environmental Conservation. Our approach is deeply rooted in the belief that local empowerment leads to lasting positive change.

We emphasize client success and apply agile methodologies in our projects, ensuring flexibility, efficiency, and measurable impact. Our commitment is to work collaboratively with community members, local businesses, and environmental partners to create vibrant, resilient, and self-sufficient coastal communities.


Small Business Conferences

SHCDP, founded Small Business Conferences focusing on one-day events, created for entrepreneurs, founders, side-hustle operators, and community business leaders who want practical guidance they can apply immediately. The conferences bring together experienced speakers, local changemakers, and small business support voices to address the everyday decisions that shape growth. Rather than offering vague motivation, the programmes are built around useful topics such as digital presence, funding readiness, leadership, compliance, and building stronger local business networks.

Our Services

At shcdp, we are dedicated to driving positive change in communities. Our core focus areas include:

Entrepreneurship Programs

Providing resources, training, and mentorship to local entrepreneurs to help them establish and grow sustainable businesses.

Skills Development Workshops

Offering practical skills training in various trades to enhance employability and create new economic opportunities for community members.

Environmental Conservation Projects

Engaging the community in initiatives aimed at preserving marine ecosystems, promoting sustainable fishing practices, and maintaining clean harbor environments.
